Optimizing Recovery Rate of Debt

The project is from PICK International Asset Management Corp.. My goal was to maximize the recovery rate of debt. Firstly, I conducted k-means clustering to look into the latent factors sharing all debtors. Secondly, I trained the Random Forest, RNN and Logistic models accordingly and decreased training time with GPU computing. Last but not least, I A/B tested the model to compare the prediction accuracy rate of them. The result ended up with about a 20% rise in profit with RNN.
